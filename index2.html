Gachiakuta

This is a Manga series about a boy from a floating city who gets cast out and falls down to the surface where the city dumps its trash. When he wakes up he is surrounded by the cities' discarded trash, he learns that some of it has begun turning into monsters made of trash. He joins a group of trash beast exterminators called The Cleaners and begins to search for a way back up to the city so he can have his revenge.

Main Characters

Rudo Surebrec : A fiery, stubborn boy from the slums who is thrown down to the surface and forced to survive. He has a strong sense of justice and the rare ability to draw power from discarded objects, turning “trash” into deadly tools.
Enjin : A laid-back but sharp-minded member of the Cleaners who takes Rudo in. He acts carefree, but he is highly skilled in combat and leadership, often hiding seriousness behind humor.
Zanka Nijiku : Loud, aggressive, and competitive, Zanka thrives on battle. He is blunt and hot-headed, but beneath that is a strong loyalty to his teammates and a drive to prove himself.
Riyo Reaper : Calm, intimidating, and brutally efficient. Riyo rarely shows emotion and lets her actions speak for her, making her one of the most feared and reliable fighters among the Cleaners.

My favorite character is Rudo Surebrec, a hot-blooded survivor cast onto the surface and labeled as trash, yet he refuses to accept a world that throws people away. Fighting to prove that worth is not defined by labels, he turns discarded objects into weapons, living by the belief: “Just because something is thrown away doesn not mean it is useless.”
